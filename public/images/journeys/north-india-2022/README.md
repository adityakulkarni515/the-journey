# North India 2022 - Photo Guide

## Required Images

Add your photos to this folder with these exact filenames:

### Hero & Thumbnail
- `hero.jpg` - Main hero image (recommended: 1920x1080 or wider)
- `thumb.jpg` - Thumbnail for cards (recommended: 800x600)

### Gallery Images
- `gallery-1.jpg` through `gallery-6.jpg`

### Moment Cards (these appear in the "Captured Moments" section)
- `moment-manikarnika.jpg` - The cremation ghat scene
- `moment-boat.jpg` - Boat ride on the Ganga
- `moment-taj.jpg` - Taj Mahal
- `moment-bhang.jpg` - Bhang thandai experience
- `moment-friends.jpg` - Photo with Prathamesh and Prajwal

### Day-wise Images (optional)
You can add day-specific images like:
- `day-1-airport.jpg`
- `day-3-boat.jpg`
- `day-5-taj.jpg`
etc.

## Image Recommendations

1. **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics
2. **Hero image**: At least 1920px wide, landscape orientation
3. **Moments**: Square or 4:3 ratio works best
4. **File size**: Compress images (use tinypng.com) for faster loading

## How Images Appear

- **Hero**: Full-width at the top of the journey page
- **Gallery**: Grid layout in the story
- **Moments**: Cards with your captions overlaid
- **Timeline**: Can be added to specific days
